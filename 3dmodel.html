3D model interaction.

Interacting with my 3d model within my website is made simple. All you need to do is to click on the play button on the still image of the 3D model. Click and drag to move the angle of view. To change the position of the 3D model you can do this by pressing down on the scroll wheel and dragging your mouse. To inlarge the image scroll forwards and backwards using the scroll wheel on your mouse.
